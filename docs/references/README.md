# Scientific References

Peer-reviewed literature and technical specifications supporting labanalysis methodology.

## Overview

This section provides the scientific foundations for biomechanical calculations, signal processing methods, predictive equations, and file format specifications implemented in labanalysis. All references are from peer-reviewed journals and authoritative textbooks.

---

## Reference Documents

### [Biomechanics References](biomechanics.md)

Scientific foundations for biomechanical analysis, including:
- **Motion analysis**: ISB coordinate system standards, marker-based kinematics
- **Anthropometry**: De Leva 1996 segment parameters
- **Gait analysis**: Perry & Burnfield spatiotemporal parameters
- **Force platforms**: Winter 2009 GRF analysis methods
- **EMG processing**: Surface electromyography standards
- **Jump mechanics**: Countermovement jump analysis

**Key papers**:
- Wu et al. (2002, 2005) - ISB joint coordinate systems (DOI: 10.1016/S0021-9290(01)00222-6, 10.1016/j.jbiomech.2004.05.042)
- Winter, D.A. (2009) - Biomechanics and Motor Control
- De Leva, P. (1996) - Segment inertia parameters (DOI: 10.1016/0021-9290(95)00178-6)
- Perry & Burnfield (2010) - Gait Analysis: Normal and Pathological Function

### [Equation References](equations.md)

Predictive equations for performance assessment:
- **Strength testing**: Brzycki 1RM, Epley, Lander formulas
- **Cardiorespiratory fitness**: ACSM VO2max equations, Astrand-Rhyming protocol
- **Anthropometry**: Body surface area (DuBois), BMI standards
- **Jump performance**: Bosco equations, flight time methods

**Key sources**:
- Brzycki, M. (1993) - 1RM prediction (Strength & Conditioning Journal)
- ACSM (2021) - Guidelines for Exercise Testing and Prescription
- Bosco et al. (1983) - Jump test mechanics (DOI: 10.1007/BF00422166)

### [File Format Specifications](file-formats.md)

Technical documentation for biomechanical data formats:
- **TDF (Time Data Format)**: BTS Bioengineering proprietary format
- **C3D**: Standard biomechanical data format (c3d.org specification)
- **OpenSim formats**: .mot, .sto, .trc file structures
- **CSV/Excel**: Tabular data export conventions

## Additional Peer-Reviewed References

### Signal Processing

**Butterworth Filtering**
- Butterworth, S. (1930). On the theory of filter amplifiers. *Wireless Engineer*, 7, 536-541.
- Winter, D.A. (2009). Chapter 2: Signal Processing. In *Biomechanics and Motor Control of Human Movement*.

**Digital Signal Processing**
- Smith, S.W. (1997). *The Scientist and Engineer's Guide to Digital Signal Processing*. California Technical Publishing.

### Biomechanical Models

**WholeBody Model**
- Dumas, R., Chèze, L., & Verriest, J.P. (2007). Adjustments to McConville et al. and Young et al. body segment inertial parameters. *Journal of Biomechanics*, 40(3), 543-553. DOI: 10.1016/j.jbiomech.2006.02.013

**Joint Kinematics**
- Grood, E.S., & Suntay, W.J. (1983). A joint coordinate system for the clinical description of three-dimensional motions. *Journal of Biomechanical Engineering*, 105(2), 136-144. DOI: 10.1115/1.3138397
- Cole, G.K., Nigg, B.M., Ronsky, J.L., & Yeadon, M.R. (1993). Application of the joint coordinate system to three-dimensional joint attitude and movement representation. *Journal of Biomechanical Engineering*, 115(4A), 344-349. DOI: 10.1115/1.2895496

### Jump Analysis

**Countermovement Jump**
- Bosco, C., Luhtanen, P., & Komi, P.V. (1983). A simple method for measurement of mechanical power in jumping. *European Journal of Applied Physiology*, 50(2), 273-282. DOI: 10.1007/BF00422166
- Linthorne, N.P. (2001). Analysis of standing vertical jumps using a force platform. *American Journal of Physics*, 69(11), 1198-1204. DOI: 10.1119/1.1397460

**Reactive Strength**
- Flanagan, E.P., & Comyns, T.M. (2008). The use of contact time and the reactive strength index to optimize fast stretch-shortening cycle training. *Strength & Conditioning Journal*, 30(5), 32-38. DOI: 10.1519/SSC.0b013e318187e25b

### Gait and Running

**Spatiotemporal Parameters**
- Whittle, M.W. (2007). *Gait Analysis: An Introduction* (4th ed.). Butterworth-Heinemann.
- Nordin, M., & Frankel, V.H. (2012). *Basic Biomechanics of the Musculoskeletal System* (4th ed.). Lippincott Williams & Wilkins.

**Running Biomechanics**

- Novacheck, T.F. (1998). The biomechanics of running. *Gait & Posture*, 7(1), 77-95. DOI: 10.1016/S0966-6362(97)00038-6

### Balance and Posture

**Center of Pressure**

- Paillard, T., & Noé, F. (2015). Techniques and methods for testing the postural function in healthy and pathological subjects. *BioMed Research International*, 2015, 891390. DOI: 10.1155/2015/891390

### Strength Assessment

**Isokinetic Testing**
- Dvir, Z. (2004). *Isokinetics: Muscle Testing, Interpretation, and Clinical Applications* (2nd ed.). Churchill Livingstone.

### VO2max and Cardiorespiratory Fitness
